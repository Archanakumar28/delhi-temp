Tracing the Temporary

A Digital Humanities Archive of Delhi’s Ephemeral Architecture

Delhi is often imagined through permanence—its monuments and ruins. Yet alongside this exists another city: one that is constantly built, transformed, and dismantled.

Festival pandals, wedding tents, protest stages, and pop-up markets reshape space, gather people, and then disappear—leaving behind memory rather than material.

This project explores these structures as cultural texts—expressions of identity, power, and belief.
