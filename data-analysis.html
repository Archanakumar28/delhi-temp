Quantitative & Spatial Data Analysis

This section extends the project into a data-driven interpretation of temporary architecture in Delhi. While individual entries in the archive capture specific moments, this page aggregates those observations to identify broader cultural and spatial patterns.

The visualisations presented here highlight how different forms of temporary architecture are distributed across function, duration, and material use. Rather than treating these structures as isolated events, the analysis reveals recurring tendencies that reflect the rhythms of ritual, celebration, political expression, and economic activity within the city.

The purpose of this section is not statistical precision, but interpretative clarity—demonstrating how digital tools can make visible patterns that are otherwise difficult to perceive in fragmented, short-lived urban phenomena.
