Quantitative & Spatial Data Analysis

This section extends the project into a data-driven interpretation of temporary architecture in Delhi. While individual entries capture specific moments, this layer identifies broader cultural and spatial patterns across the city.

These visualisations map recurring tendencies in function, duration, and material usage, revealing how temporary structures align with cycles of ritual, celebration, political expression, and economic activity.

Rather than offering statistical finality, this analysis provides interpretative clarity—demonstrating how digital tools can make visible patterns within ephemeral urban phenomena.
